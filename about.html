Our Story

Cultivating passion for the underwater world, one plant at a time.

Born from a Love for Nature

Aquafix started as a small hobby project in Colombo and has grown into a premier destination for aquatic plant enthusiasts. We believe that an aquarium is more than just a glass box; it's a living, breathing ecosystem that brings tranquility and beauty into your space.

Our mission is to provide the highest quality, pest-free aquatic plants to hobbyists across Sri Lanka. Whether you are building a high-tech Iwagumi layout or a simple low-tech community tank, we have the plants and expertise to support your journey.
